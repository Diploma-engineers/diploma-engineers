📌 BHEL / BEL Apprentice क्या है?

Diploma Engineers के लिए BHEL (Bharat Heavy Electricals Limited) और BEL (Bharat Electronics Limited) Apprentice Programs एक अच्छा Career Start Option हैं। इन Programs में Industry Training, Practical Exposure और Government PSU Environment में काम सीखने का अवसर मिलता है।

🏢 Major Work Areas

  • Power Plant Equipment
  • Electronics Systems
  • Manufacturing Division
  • Quality Control Department
  • Production Unit
  • Testing & Maintenance

🏗 Eligible Diploma Branches

  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Electrical Engineering
  • Electronics Engineering
  • Computer Science Engineering
  • Civil Engineering
  • Instrumentation Engineering
