📌 DRDO CEPTAM क्या है?

DRDO CEPTAM (Centre for Personnel Talent Management) के माध्यम से Defence Research Laboratories और Technical Departments में भर्ती होती है। Diploma Engineers के लिए Technical Assistant, Technician और कई अन्य Posts आती हैं।

🏢 Major Departments

  • Missile Development Division
  • Electronics Research Labs
  • Naval Systems
  • Aeronautics Division
  • Combat Vehicle Research
  • Defence Materials Division

🏗 Eligible Diploma Branches

  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Electrical Engineering
  • Electronics Engineering
  • Computer Science Engineering
  • Civil Engineering
  • Chemical Engineering
