📌 ISRO Technical Assistant क्या है?

ISRO (Indian Space Research Organisation) भारत की Space Agency है। Diploma Engineers के लिए Technical Assistant, Technician, Scientific Assistant और अन्य Technical Posts निकलती हैं। ISRO Jobs में High Technology Environment, Research Projects और Career Growth मिलता है।

🏢 Major ISRO Centres

  • VSSC (Vikram Sarabhai Space Centre)
  • SDSC (Satish Dhawan Space Centre)
  • LPSC (Liquid Propulsion Systems Centre)
  • ISAC
  • ISTRAC
  • Space Applications Centre

🏗 Eligible Diploma Branches

  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Electrical Engineering
  • Electronics Engineering
  • Computer Science Engineering
  • Civil Engineering
  • Instrumentation Engineering
