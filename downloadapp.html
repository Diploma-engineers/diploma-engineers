About this app

Diploma Engineers is an educational platform specially designed for diploma students. This app provides high-quality Notes, E-Books, Previous Year Questions (PYQ), Practical Files, Study Materials, Important Updates, and Exam Notifications for all diploma branches.

Students can easily access:
✔ Civil Engineering Notes
✔ Mechanical Engineering Study Material
✔ Electrical Engineering PDFs
✔ Computer Science Notes
✔ Previous Year Question Papers
✔ Semester Wise Content
✔ Practical Files & Viva Questions
✔ Latest BTEUP Updates

The app is simple, fast, lightweight, and specially made for diploma students to improve learning experience.
